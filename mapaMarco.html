Correlation between air pollution and bus traffic

This map shows that sensors located very close to high bus traffic streets (50m radius) has a bigger gap between the lowest and the highest air pollution values.

The circle radius shows how big was the daily gap between lowest and highest air pollution value.

Without bus traffic near the sensor, the circle shows a solid green colour.

More the traffic, reddish the circle become. The bus traffic value is normalized with its week peak.

Data collected between 07/03/2015 and 13/01/2015, a "cool" summer week in Rio, with natural inversion phenomenon. When it happens, the air pollutants become trapped between a low cool atmospheric layer and a high hot atmospheric layer. In this scenario, the whole city is affected with decreased pollution dispersion. For clarification, please compare it with the hot week map.
Clicking on the marks gives some info about the sensor and the collected data.
